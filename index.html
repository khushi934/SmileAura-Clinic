Dental Selants

Dental sealants provide a protective shield over your teeth to prevent cavities. This quick, painless treatment is especially helpful for children and adults with deep grooves in their molars. Sealants help keep your teeth strong, healthy, and protected for years.

Dental Dictionary

Plaque – A soft layer of bacteria on teeth that can cause cavities. Tartar – Hardened plaque that needs professional cleaning. Cavities – Small holes in teeth caused by bacteria. Enamel – The hard outer layer that protects the tooth. Gingivitis – Early gum infection that causes bleeding and swelling. Root Canal – A treatment to save an infected tooth. Crowns – Caps placed on damaged teeth to restore strength. etc.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a permanent and natural-looking solution for missing teeth. They replace both the root and the tooth, providing strong support, improved chewing, and long-lasting comfort. With advanced techniques, we ensure safe, painless, and reliable implant placement for a confident smile.
